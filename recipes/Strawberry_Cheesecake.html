Strawberry Cheesecake in a Jar

Photo by Olga Petnyunene on Unsplash

Delicious no-bake strawberry cheesecakes prepared in jars.

They need to be stored in the refrigerator. Some of the strawberries can be blended up and mixed into the cheesecake mix. This guarantees some strawberry in each bite. The crust can be made with Oreo cookies as well.

Ingredients

    Crust:

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 4 half pint canning jars with lids and rings

    • Cheesecake:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 fresh strawberries, sliced

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Prepare crust: Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 1/4 cup sugar together in a bowl. Press 1/4 of mixture into the bottom of each jar.
  3. Prepare cheesecake: Beat cream cheese and whipped topping together with an electric mixer in a bowl until well blended. Beat 2/3 cup sugar and vanilla extract into cream cheese mixture until smooth and fluffy.
  4. Spoon 1/4 of cream cheese mixture onto graham cracker crust in each jar.
  5. Top each cheesecake with sliced strawberries. Refrigerate cheesecakes until serving time. Enjoy!
